AFC-500

AFC-500: Open Thermal Architecture for SPCW

A field-built 500 mm chamber designed to demonstrate controlled gas-phase oxidation of synthetic polymer compound waste (SPCW) through a simple but behavior-driven structure.

Overview

AFC-500 is the demonstration platform of the Post-Incineration Framework. It is intended to show how chamber geometry, air layering, and reaction behavior can produce stable gas-phase oxidation in an open thermal treatment system.
