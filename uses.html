Recommended Uses

This section outlines the recommended applications of the proposed polymer compounds incineration technology. The use cases focus on research, experimental deployment, and environmentally controlled scenarios.

Non-Commercial Research

The technology is well-suited for academic and institutional research where experimental validation of advanced incineration methods is required. It may be used to analyze emission reduction efficiency and thermal stability.

Environmental Testing Facilities

Controlled testing environments such as environmental laboratories or pilot-scale waste treatment facilities can utilize this method to evaluate pollutant suppression under regulated conditions.

Regions Without Patent Restrictions

In regions where no related patents have been filed, the technology may be applied for educational demonstrations or public-sector waste treatment research initiatives.

Available locations

  • Island areas
  • Military facilities
  • Remote hospitals
  • Marine waste collection vessel - small size
  • Marine waste collection vessel - large size
  • WTE
  • Remote areas where waste transport is not easy
  • Separate incineration of combustible waste (in countries where permitted)
