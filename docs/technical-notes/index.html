Technical Notes

This section contains technical reference notes that support field trials and community-based validation. These documents are not marketing material, nor finalized specifications. They are working notes intended to be read, questioned, and reused.

Scope of these notes

The documents collected here describe technical considerations, operational boundaries, and design principles related to appropriate incineration approaches.

  • Combustion and airflow principles
  • Operational constraints and failure modes
  • Emission-related considerations and limits
  • Measurement assumptions and uncertainties
  • Practical safety and maintenance observations
